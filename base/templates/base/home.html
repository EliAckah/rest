Worldwide non-profit charity organization

Rest Charity Foundation is a non-profit organization in Sunyani, Ghana, West Africa seeking to give rest to restless and hope to the hopeless.
To improve the quality of lives for the less privileged in education, health and nutrition, the homeless and the financially handicapped through fundraising initiatives and volunteered support and transform our communities by improving these lives.
Rest Charity Foundation seeks to bring rest to the restless and hope to the despairing heart.

What We Do?

We believe that we can save more lifes with you

Healthy Food

Provide healthy foods for nursing mothers and their babies, street beggars and to those who can't afford

Social Care

Provide devices for the disabled, make donations to orphanges, make donations prisons, make donations the center for the aged.

Health Care

Pay hospital bills for those who need intensive care and can not afford.

Primary Education

Provide schools with educational stationary and pay the school fees of needy but brilliant students.

Residence Facilities

Provide shelters for the homeless.
