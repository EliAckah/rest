Rest Charity Foundation is a non-profit organization in Sunyani, Ghana, West Africa seeking to give rest to restless and hope to the hopeless.
To improve the quality of lives for the less privileged in education, health and nutrition, the homeless and the financially handicapped through fundraising initiatives and volunteered support and transform our communities by improving these lives.
Rest Charity Foundation seeks to bring rest to the restless and hope to the despairing heart.
